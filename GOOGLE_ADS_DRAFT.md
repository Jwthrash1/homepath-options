# HomePath Options Google Search Campaign Draft

This is a cautious starter campaign for lead generation. Have mortgage compliance counsel review ad copy, landing page copy, disclosures, and targeting before launch.

## Campaign Settings

Campaign type: Search

Goal: Leads

Landing page:

```text
https://homepathoptions.com
```

Initial geography:

```text
United States
```

Recommended first test budget:

```text
$25 to $50 per day
```

Bidding:

```text
Start with Maximize Clicks or Maximize Conversions after conversion data is available.
```

Conversion:

```text
generate_lead
```

## Compliance Guardrails

Do not claim:

- Guaranteed approval
- Lowest rates
- Government affiliation
- No-cost loans
- Specific savings
- Specific payment amounts
- Credit approval likelihood
- Reverse mortgage as free money or a government benefit

Use:

- Licensed mortgage professional
- Options
- Information
- Request
- Connect
- No obligation

## First 7-Day Test Plan

Day 1:

- Launch only HELOC and Refinance ad groups.
- Budget: $25/day.
- Watch for disapprovals.

Days 2-3:

- Pause any keyword spending without relevant clicks.
- Add negative keywords from search terms.

Days 4-7:

- Review cost per lead, form completion, and Monday lead quality.
- Only expand budget if leads are valid and contactable.

## Pre-Launch Checklist

- Legal pages reviewed.
- Business address added if available.
- Google Analytics key event configured.
- Monday test lead confirmed.
- Search terms reviewed daily during first week.
