# HomePath Options Launch Checklist

Use this before sending real traffic from paid ads, social media, or referral partners.

## Lead Capture

- CRM or backup lead delivery is turned on.
- Test lead submitted from desktop.
- Test lead submitted from cellular phone.
- Lead arrives with name, email, phone, state, ZIP, loan goal, score, and consent date.
- You know where the lead appears in the CRM.
- You know how to follow up with the lead.

## Website

- HomePathOptions.com loads correctly.
- Mobile homepage looks good.
- Lead form works from phone.
- Thank-you message appears after submission.
- Phone number and email are correct.
- Legal/disclosure language is visible.
- Privacy Policy and Terms pages work.

## Tracking

- Google Analytics shows site visitors.
- Google Analytics shows lead events.
- Paid outlet uses the tracking link.
- Google Ads uses the correct final URL.
- Weekly numbers are recorded.

## Compliance

- No ad promises guaranteed approval.
- No ad promises lowest rates.
- No ad says everyone qualifies.
- No ad says cash today.
- No ad implies government affiliation.
- No ad says free money.
- Social ads for credit or housing use the required special ad category when applicable.

## Follow-Up Process

- New lead is contacted quickly.
- Missed leads get a second follow-up.
- You track which source produced each lead.
- You track whether the lead was sold, contacted, or unqualified.
- You review lead quality weekly.

## First Launch Goal

Start small.

First target:

- 100 website visitors
- 10 completed leads
- 3 to 5 strong leads
- Clear source tracking
- One working referral partner
