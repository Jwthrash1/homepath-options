Routing

          
Partner criteria

        

        

          

            
Purchase

            
Route by state, timeline, purchase goal, and credit range. Prioritize 0 to 90 day buyers.

          

          

            
Refinance and HELOC

            
Route by estimated equity, mortgage balance, cash-out intent, and urgency.

          

          

            
Reverse mortgage

            
Route only when the homeowner age is 62 or older and the state is supported by a qualified partner.

          

        

      


      

        

          
Safeguards

          
Advertising and consent controls

        

        

          

            
No rate promises

            
Do not advertise payments, rates, savings, government affiliation, or approval likelihood unless disclosures and substantiation are ready.

          

          

            
Consent evidence

            
Store consent language, timestamp, page URL, IP address, partner name, source campaign, and submitted answers.

          

          

            
Licensed handoff

            
Do not offer or negotiate loan terms in this funnel. Route qualified consumers to licensed mortgage professionals.

          

          

            
Reverse mortgage care

            
Use age gates, avoid benefit-style framing, and keep educational language clear and balanced.

          

        

      

    


    

      

        HomePath Options is a lead intake concept. It is not a lender and does not make credit decisions. All financing discussions must be handled by licensed mortgage professionals.
